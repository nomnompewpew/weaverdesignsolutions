Search Engine Optimization

Getting good rankings on search engines is about creating quality content that is relevant to the end user, and getting that content indexed by the major search engines. The more clicks you get for a given search term that results in a conversion, the more likely Google will up your ranking. Connecting and using the available Google tools like Google My Business, Search Console, Analytics and Ads is the best way to get ranked quickly and start getting conversions that translate to sales or foot traffic to your business.

With the Google suite of tools I can set a monthly budget for ads, setup reporting and track progress as Google optimizes your search terms to connect potential customers with your business.
