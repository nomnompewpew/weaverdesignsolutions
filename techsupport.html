IT Services

Tech Support and Consulting

With over 10 years in the industry and a passion for technology, I can tackle a variety of OS builds, networks and technologies. Whether you are a business or individual struggling to learn a new barcode management system, point of sale system or even just setting up a printer - I can help! There are a lot of brands, devices and softwares to choose from and I can help you understand how they will work together. If you've ever had a tech issue involving multiple companies, you know how frustrating it is to be bounced back and forth between them to get the information you need. I am here to connect the dots in the process of buying, setting up and empowering you to maintain your systems without hiring a full time IT person.

My interest in technology started at a very early age as home computers and the internet became popular. I have strong interests that bring me to the great outdoors of Idaho, but I have always been drawn to computers as a form of entertainment, education and even art. Whether working as a mason, maintenance man, framer, call center supervisor or service worker I have always had a computer and tinkered with technology.

At 23, I landed a job doing consumer support for an industry leading computer and device manufacturer. During my tenure of almost 9 years I worked on tablets, mobile phones, computers, networks and network attached devices. After 2 years as a technical support advisor, I was promoted to a supervisor/management position where I led a team of 15-20 tech support advisors.

During my off-time at my previous job I did things like modifying game consoles, building custom gaming computers, recording music, jailbreaking/rooting my phones and doing free coding courses online to increase my knowledge and ability to use technology for practical applications. I built my first website for my band and my bandmates and I used our knowledge of technology to record some of our first music.

One of my bandmates had a business idea and asked if I could help build a website, write some employee handbooks and and do a handful of other IT, admin, HR and even sales tasks. It was a slow build, but soon it made more sense for me to leave my job to pursue this business full time. Thanks to my experience with TVHF LLC, I learned how to apply for a business license, get a Federal and State Tax ID, purchase workers comp, do payroll and taxes and find a way to creatively solve every problem that may come up with a new business.

Technologies I've worked with, supported or setup for a business:
Android, iOS, MacOS, Windows, Microsoft Office Suite, Apple iWork suite, Apple Networks and Multimedia, Google Nest/Home, QuickBooks/TurboTax/Intuit, Wireless Printers, Wasp Barcode and Inventory System, ShippingEasy, Ecwid, Shopify, Security Cameras, Google Analytics/SEO, email, networks and internet, cloud sharing, remote connectivity and much more! 

My advertised price of $40.00 per hour will apply to most situations, but I will consider a flat rate fee for some jobs.
