Questions and Answers

What technologies do I use?

I like to build websites using the Google Domains platform served by a static Github repository. This approach offers a simple way to integrate email addresses that end in your domain name, easy setup of Google ads, SEO, and reporting. I am familiar with setting up the Ecwid shopping cart platform and can setup any other like Shopify, WooCommerce, Opencart and more. My websites are built in an offline builder and published to the respository for debugging before going live. Most websites can be deployed in as little as one to two days and updates can be made in just hours. Shopping cart, scheduling and shipping integrations have associated CMS platforms that you will have admin access to create new products, create events, run promotions and otherwise manage the content within your site without always needing me.

How long will it take?

I will always let my clients know of my current workload and when I can start on your site. Once I've started, I will focus on your project until completion. Many websites, SEO, adwork and social media work will take just one day. After that I can continue to drive you better search results, monitor your website's health and send information about demographics and sales conversions with automated reports.

What do I need from you?

I will need the highest resolution/quality of your logos, pictures, graphics, menus or any other media you would like on your site. Provide me with any written content you would like to include and I will do the rest!

Can I add/update content on my own?

Yes. I work with various plugins that have an associated CMS (content management system) that will allow an administrator user with no knowledge of coding to add products, update/add pictures or share social media updates on your website without my help. I am also available to help with first time setup of your drag and drop site, social media platforms or even teach you enough to make updates with some of the professional tools I use.
