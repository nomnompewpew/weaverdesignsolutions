Affordable Solutions with Great Results

It's tough navigating the wealth of options out there for building your website or brand presence online. For many businesses, a website may be less important than an active Instagram with rich content, a nice looking Facebook page or a Google my Business profile with excellent looking pictures, contact information or menus.

A website may be the final touch that wraps all these things together in a nice press kit for your customers. All of my websites come with three great looking responsive versions of your site for desktop, mobile or tablet. This means your customer will have a great experience on your site no matter what device they are using and you won't be left with partially cut off pages or incorrectly sized videos or pictures. Whatever it is you are looking for, I will find the most affordable way to deliver what your business needs to drive more customers to your brick and mortar store, or to make online sales and shipping more seamless and professional.

My focus is delivering a high quality service without hidden monthly costs or expensive updates. I am happy to build your drag and drop site and manage it for you, but these site builders tend to have apps or plugins that cost monthly for each little feature you want to add. The solutions I come up with generally require more setup or even a bit of coding, but they come at a much lower cost (sometimes free) than drag and drop site builders.

Questions and Answers

What technologies do I use?

I like to build websites using the Google Domains platform served by a static Github repository. This approach offers a simple way to integrate email addresses that end in your domain name, easy setup of Google ads, SEO, and reporting. I am familiar with setting up the Ecwid shopping cart platform and can setup any other like Shopify, WooCommerce, Opencart and more. My websites are built in an offline builder and published to the respository for debugging before going live. Most websites can be deployed in as little as one to two days and updates can be made in just hours.

How long will it take?

I will always let my clients know of my current workload and when I can start on your site. Once I've started, I will focus on your project until completion. Many websites, SEO, adwork and social media work will take just one day. After that I can continue to drive you better search results, monitor your website's health and send information about demographics and sales conversions with automated reports.

What do I need from you?

I will need the highest resolution/quality of your logos, pictures, graphics, menus or any other media you would like on your site. Provide me with any written content you would like to include and I will do the rest!

Can I add/update content on my own?

Yes. I work with various plugins that will allow an administrator user with no knowledge of coding to add products, update/add pictures or share social media updates on your website without my help. I am also available to help with first time setup of your drag and drop site, social media platforms or even teach you enough to make updates with some of the professional tools I use.
