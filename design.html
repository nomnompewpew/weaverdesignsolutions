Search Engine Optimization

Getting good rankings on search engines is about creating quality content that is relevant to the end user, and getting that content indexed by the major search engines. The more clicks you get for a given search term that results in a conversion, the more likely Google will up your ranking. Connecting and using the available Google tools like Google My Business, Search Console, Analytics and Ads is the best way to get ranked quickly and start getting conversions that translate to sales or foot traffic to your business.

With the Google suite of tools I can set a monthly budget for ads, setup reporting and track progress as Google optimizes your search terms to connect potential customers with your business.

Marketing                                

A good online marketing plan should drive customers to your brick and mortar or online store, generate reviews and continue to create more interest in your business. There is a variety of ways to go about this including samples, promotions, social media influencers and purchased ad space to get your name out there to be reviewed. It is up to you to provide excellent customer service and a great product to get those awesome reviews. Once you are reviewed, customers will be much more likely to stop in for the first time and those who were provided samples or promotions will likely return for repeat business.

If you are making online sales or a POS that can be tracked by customer name, we can track the rate of ad spend or organic searches into sales conversions.
